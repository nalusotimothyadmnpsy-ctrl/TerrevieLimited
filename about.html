Who We Are

About Terrieve

TerreVie Limited is a youth-led business dedicated to transforming cassava into premium, nutritious products while creating sustainable livelihoods for farmers and communities.

› About Us
Our Story

Built on a Vision of Transformation

Terrieve was founded to unlock the immense potential of cassava in our region. We integrate farming, outgrower partnerships and modern processing to deliver high-quality cassava products for local and regional markets.

We are committed to innovation, sustainability and empowering farmers while driving industrial development and food security. From a single processing unit, we have grown into a vertically integrated agro-processing enterprise with thousands of partner farmers.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality cassava products through sustainable practices that create value for our customers and communities across the region.

Our Vision

To become the leading cassava processor and a key contributor to agricultural transformation and food security — empowering Africa from the farm up.

Our Values

  • Innovation in processes and products
  • Sustainability for future generations
  • Quality above all else
  • Partnership with farmers and industry
