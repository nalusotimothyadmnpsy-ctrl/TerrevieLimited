Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get asked most.

Q How do I become an outgrower farmer?

Simply contact us through this form or call our office. Our agronomy team will schedule a visit, assess your farm and walk you through the registration and onboarding process.

Q What is the minimum order quantity for products?

Minimum order quantities vary by product. For HQCF, minimum orders start at one metric tonne. Please contact our sales team for detailed pricing and MOQ information.

Q Do you offer product samples?

Yes, we provide product samples for qualified buyers and industry partners. Request a sample through our contact form specifying the product of interest.

Q Can you supply custom product formulations?

Absolutely. We work with businesses to develop tailored cassava-based formulations. Reach out to our technical team to discuss your specific requirements and application needs.

Q What certifications do your products hold?

Our products meet national quality standards. We are currently pursuing ISO and food safety certifications. Certificates of analysis are provided with every order shipment.

Q Do you deliver or do buyers collect?

We offer both delivery and ex-factory collection options. Delivery logistics and pricing depend on order size and destination. Contact us for a delivery quote.
